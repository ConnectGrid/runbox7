Email Aliases

Here you can set up and configure aliases for your main Runbox account.

An alias can be another Runbox address or an address on your own domain, if you have any set up under Email Domains. Please allow up to 5 minutes for new aliases to propagate throughout the Runbox servers before using them, and please avoid aliases that could be perceived to be official Runbox administrative addresses (they may be deleted without prior notice).

Note: Avoid aliases that could be perceived to be official Runbox addresses or ones used by other companies or individuals, as they may be deleted without prior notice.
